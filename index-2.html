About the Doctor

Meet Your Trusted
Physiotherapist

Dr. Krishna Devaliya is a qualified physiotherapist with a Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) and over 2 years of clinical experience. He is currently pursuing his MPT (Master of Physiotherapy) to further enhance his expertise. His passion for patient care, combined with his commitment to continuous learning, makes him a dynamic and dedicated healthcare professional.

Dr. Krishna believes every patient deserves a treatment plan built specifically for them — not a generic protocol. He combines modern clinical techniques with a warm, patient-first approach, ensuring each individual feels heard, supported, and confident in their recovery journey.

Pain Management

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Specialized recovery programs for athletes and active individuals — from sprains and strains to ligament injuries, designed for a full return to performance.

Post-Surgery Recovery

Safe, guided rehabilitation following orthopedic or other surgeries. We help you regain strength, mobility, and independence as quickly as possible.

Neurological Physiotherapy
